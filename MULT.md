## Contest Information
- **Dates:** 2nd Sunday of January (2026: January 11)
- **SSB:** 0600-0800 UTC
- **CW:** 0830-1030 UTC
- **Bands:** 80m and 40m only
- **Scoring:** QSO points × multipliers (per band, per mode)
